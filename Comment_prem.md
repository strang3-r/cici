| Person | Comment on Preamble |
|---|---|
| **Jawaharlal Nehru** | "The Preamble is the soul of the Constitution. It reflects the dreams and aspirations of the people of India. It is the bedrock upon which the entire edifice of our Constitution is built." |
| **B. R. Ambedkar** | "The Preamble expresses the fundamental principles and ideals that the Constitution seeks to achieve. It is the key to the Constitution and the spirit of the Constitution. It is the first expression of the Indian people about their intention to form a Sovereign Democratic Republic." |
| **Sir Alladi Krishnaswami Ayyar** | "The Preamble is a part of the Constitution and is of the utmost importance. It is the key to the minds of the Constitution-makers and will be of great importance in interpreting the Constitution." |
| **K. M. Munshi** | "The Preamble is the horoscope of our sovereign democratic republic. It is a statement of our cherished beliefs and the fundamental principles of our Constitution. It is a declaration of our faith in the people and in the future of our country." |
| **Granville Austin** | "The Preamble is not merely a statement of objectives, but a commitment to justice, liberty, equality, and fraternity. It enshrines the core values that bind the Indian nation together." |
| **Sir Ernest Barker** | "Preamble as the 'Key-Note' to the Constitution." |
