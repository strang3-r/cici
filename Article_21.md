### The Key Phrase: "Procedure Established by Law" ğŸ“œ

This is where the **rule of law** comes into play. It means that if the state wants to deprive someone of their life or liberty, it must follow a **fair, just, and reasonable procedure** that is laid down by a valid law.

This is different from the American concept of "due process of law," which is more expansive. However, Indian courts have often interpreted "procedure established by law" to include principles of natural justice, making it very close to "due process."
